My Education

"SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN DATO MAHARAJA LELA"


This is my first school. I went to school at Dato' Maharaja Lela School from the age of 7 to 12 years old. This school is very close to my house. It only takes 5 minutes if riding a vehicle such as a motorcycle or car.This school is big, because almost all the residents of the 3 villages who send their children to study in this school. Each grade has 7 to 8 classes. This primary school is the same as other primary schools which has 2 sessions. Students in grades 4, 5 and 6 attend the morning session, while students in grades 1, 2 and 3 attend the afternoon session.

Primary school is the best. Childhood, there is no need to think about adult things. No need to think about money, about bills, about anything. That is a child, i only knows how to play. At that time, i was not mature yet i was naughty.For your information, I am an excellent student, you know. 1st grade time, I was randomly admitted to 1st grade. Greetings. 5th ranked class at the school. After I sat for a compulsory test, I was admitted to class 1 Adnin, class number 2. I was in class Adnin until grade 5. After working hard, I managed to get into class number 1 in grade 6 which is class Firdaus.And finally, I was also one of those who managed to make my school famous, by getting 5 A's in the UPSR results. I am so happy and proud of myself.

"SEKOLAH AGAMA MENENGAH JERAM"


Jeram Secondary Religious School is a school that has many memories for me. That's also where I know the meaning of love. Love has become a common thing, especially teenagers will definitely not get away with love.

Every day I go to school by bus. I have to get out of the house waiting for the bus at 6:30 am and will arrive exactly at 6:50 am at school. Memories of riding the bus crowded with the students I will never forget. my grief of studying at SAMJ is also the most beautiful memory. If I could turn back time, I would stay in high school. But I know it's impossible. Life has to go on because time is always running out.
